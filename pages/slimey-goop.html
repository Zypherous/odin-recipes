Slimey Goop

Ever want something thats just slimey yet goopy? Look no further, this recipe will have slime and goop dripping out of your mouth

Ingredients

  • 32oz containers of elmers glue
  • 32oz containers of Borax (Sodium Tetraborate)
  • 32oz Dihydorgen Monoxide
  • Food Coloring of your choice, but purple preferred

Instructions

  1. The first step is to mix everything inside a large mixing bowl.
  2. Once it gets thick (approximately 10 mins), throw the spatuala out the window and get your hands in there
  3. After another 10 minutes of mixing, test the consistency by grabbing a wallop of the slimey goop, let it drip from your hand. It should take 5 seconds to hit the bowl, thats when its ready to eat.
  4. Enjoy!
