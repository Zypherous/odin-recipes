Chemical Concoction

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup H2O
  • 2/3 cup purple Fabuloso
  • 1/4 cup clorox
  • vinegar to taste
  1. The first step and final step is to pour all the ingredients into a large cup.
  2. Enjoy! Bonus flavor if you clean some dirty floors with a mini mop using the ingredients then wring it back into the cup.
